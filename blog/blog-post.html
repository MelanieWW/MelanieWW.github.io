Description

Pokémon GO Mewtwo is a legendary. Psychic type Pokemon with a max CP of 4724, 300 attack, 182 defense and 214 stamina in Pokemon GO. It was originally found in the Kanto region (Gen 1). Mewtwo is vulnerable to Bug, Dark and Ghost type moves. Mewtwo is boosted by Windy weather.

How to Catch One

You will be invited to this Pokemon GO Mewtwo exclusive raid but only if you have recently ("past few weeks") completed a raid at the Gym where the now exclusive Pokemon raid is taking place. Raid Invitations will be given out in advance with the following information

  • Pokestop name and Directions
  • Date and Time of the EX Raid
  • An icon of the Exclusive Raid Pass

This should give players plenty of time to organize and prepare themselves.

Best Moveset for Mewtwo

The best moves for Mewtwo are Confusion and Psystrike when attacking Pokémon in Gyms. This move combination has the highest total DPS and is also the best moveset for PVP battles.
